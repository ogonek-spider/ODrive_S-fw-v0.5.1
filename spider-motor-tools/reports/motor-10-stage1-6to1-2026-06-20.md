# Motor #10 — first-stage (1:6) gearbox free-spin (2026-06-20)

Follow-up to `motor-10-health-2026-06-20.md`. The full **36:1** gearbox was found
binding (stalled at 13 A on a 10 t/s command). The 36:1 is two 6:1 stages; this
test isolates the fault by running **only the first 1:6 stage** attached.

- ODrive serial 3482345a3034, vbus ~30.8 V, axis0 AS5047P (mode 257), offset pinned 0.95.
- Tool: `motor_health_check.py --skip-motorcal --skip-offset` (free-spin sweep only).
- Reports: `motor-10-stage1-6to1-freespin-2026-06-20.json`,
  `motor-10-stage1-6to1-freespin-highspeed-2026-06-20.json`.

## Result: first 1:6 stage is HEALTHY (not the source of binding)

| cmd (motor t/s) | 36:1 full (binding) | 1:6 stage-1 | bare motor |
|---|---|---|---|
| 5  | 3.21 A / 7.14 A → 3.35 t/s | **1.45 A / 2.39 A → 5.20 t/s ✅** | 1.63 A / 3.34 A → 4.66 t/s |
| 10 | 13.07 A / 15.14 A → **STALLED** | **1.26–1.51 A / ~3.0 A → 10.1–10.2 t/s ✅** | 2.11 A / 8.00 A → 10.07 t/s |
| 15 | — | **1.32 A / 3.33 A → 15.30 t/s ✅** | — |
| 20 | — | 1.44 A / 3.02 A → 15.9 t/s (voltage-limited, not load) | — |

- Stage 1 draws as little as the bare motor (~1.3–1.5 A mean, <3.4 A peak) and
  reaches commanded speed cleanly through 15 t/s.
- The 20 t/s shortfall is the **bus-voltage speed ceiling** (~16 t/s motor-shaft
  at 30.8 V), not load — current stayed low, no struggle.

## Conclusion

The 36:1 binding is **not** in the first reduction stage. It lives in the
**second 6:1 stage** (or the full-assembly coupling/alignment). Next: test the
second stage standalone (or refit it) and re-run the same sweep — a current jump
back toward the 36:1 numbers confirms stage 2 as the culprit.

All config RAM-only; nothing written to flash.
